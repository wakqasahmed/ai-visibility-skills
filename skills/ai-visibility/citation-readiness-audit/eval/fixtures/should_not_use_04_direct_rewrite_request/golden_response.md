whether each one's canonical URL is stable/crawlable/sourced, and
recommends page fixes. It can't write the final replacement privacy-policy
copy for you; that's a legal/copywriting task, and privacy policy language
in particular should go through legal review, not be generated wholesale by
an audit tool.
